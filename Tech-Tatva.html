Manipal University Hackathon


  • Manipal Academy of Higher Education always works towards providing quality higher education.
  • Around 28,000 students from 57 different nations live, learn and play in the sprawling University town, nestled on a Karnataka’s Udupi district.
  • The institute has around 2500 faculty members and almost 10,000 other support and service staff, who cater towards various professional institutions in health sciences, engineering, communication, management, and humanities which dot the Wi-Fi-enabled campus.
  • The University has off-campuses in Mangalore and Bangalore, and off-shore campuses in Dubai (UAE) and Melaka (Malaysia).
  • Manipal institute provides the educational facilities like the Simulation Lab with computer-driven Manikins, an Innovation Centre, one of Asia’s largest health sciences libraries, one of the world’s best anatomy museums, a business incubation center and various other facilities.
  • The institute has various academic collaborations and twinning programs with the universities of US, UK, Australia, and other countries so that students can get the right international exposure.
  • In 2007- 2008, The institute has won the IMC Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Award and the International Asia Pacific Quality Award.
  • The Manipal Group owns and operates many campuses in India, and in Nepal, Malaysia, and Antigua, in the Caribbean.
